PHASE 2: BATTLE AND THE DEFINITION OF WINNERS AND LOOSERS


At this stage, the troops on the battlefield will be counted and the outcome of the battle will be determined according to the following rules:

Each Persian on the battlefield is worth 1 BP and receive 1 BTL
Each Immortal on the battlefield is worth 100 BP and receive 2000 BTL
Each Spartan on the battlefield is worth 1000 BP and receive 1000 BTL
Each Athenian on the battlefield is worth 100 BP and receive 2000 BTL

The outcome of the match will determined comparing BP of the two factions: the faction that gains the highest number of BP will win the challenge.

BP: Battle Points

BTL: Battle Token

PHASE 3: PRIZE AWARDING (31 October 2017)


Each participant of the winning faction will receive back 90% of his/her warriors (tokens). The remaining 10% of warriors are considered casualties, and they will be locked forever in the smart contract as dead bodies on the battlefield.

Each winning participant will also receive enemy warriors (tokens of the opposite side) as slaves in proportion to their contribution on the battlefield.
